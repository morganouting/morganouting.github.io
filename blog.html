12 November 2016

Today, I became a newly initiated member of Pi Beta Phi Fraternity for Women! It was a lovely ceremony, and I'm very happy to be a sister. I went through formal recruitment at the beginning of the semester, and I know I made the right choice in joining Pi Phi. Some fun facts are that we were founded on April 28, 1867 at Monmouth College in Monmouth, Illinois. Our sorority was founded before the term 'sorority' was even coined, which is why we are known as a fraternity for women! Children's literacy is our philanthropy, and every Friday, we read with children at the local YMCA.
